Get to Know Us

We Craft World Class-Champions

Daja United is a football club established in 2024. The club was founded with the vision of promoting football talent and fostering a strong sense of community through the sport. Since its inception, Daja United has quickly made a name for itself in the local football scene, attracting passionate players and dedicated fans

As Daja United continues to grow, it remains dedicated to nurturing young talent and providing a platform for players to develop their skills and achieve their full potential. The club's future looks bright, with aspirations of reaching higher levels of competition and making a lasting impact on the football world.

Sonubi Abayomi
Club Director
